About Digital Notice Board & Event Calendar

The Digital Notice Board & Event Calendar is a centralized online platform for publishing academic notices, circulars, and events. Administrators can post announcements categorized by department, ensuring relevant information reaches the right audience.

Important notices can be marked with an Urgent tag for better visibility. With categorization and filtering options, students can quickly search and view notices, reducing the risk of missing important updates compared to traditional physical notice boards.

About The Platform

The Digital Notice Board & Event Calendar is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline communication within educational institutions. It replaces traditional notice boards with a dynamic, real-time digital interface that is accessible to students, faculty, and administrators.
